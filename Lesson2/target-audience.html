Goals and Tasks:

Want to increase rate of protection in the environment

He spends his time:

  • Overseeing day to day operations of the facility.
  • Providing site safety and security; devise and implement site policies and procedures.
  • Conducting regular inspections and maintenance of systems and equipment; monitor fire alarm control panels and other emergency equipment.

Scenarios

  • She needs to know what the weather will be like in the afternoon in other to power the wind mill.
  • She needs to create a new schedule for workers next day so she need to know how the weather will be.
  • She need to know when it will rain so as to make contribution to the manager on when to start irrigation.
  • Farmers in sector B wants to start fertilizing tomorrow and she wants to know if it will rain then
  • The grains need to be sun-dried and she wants to know how long the sun will shine today.
